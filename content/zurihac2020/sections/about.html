ZuriHac 2020 - Online Edition

Hi Haskell friends!

So holding an event for 500 people in a physical location is clearly not an option this year. However, we are happy to have the hackathon in an online version instead, and we're very eager to provide you with a lot of the things that make ZuriHac a great time for everybody involved.

What will this look like?

  • We are streaming the talks live on YouTube. We will even hold the usual Q&A at the end, where our friendly moderators will pass your questions from the chat to the speaker.
  • We will use Discord as a digital space where we can collaborate, hack, hold lightning talks, or simply have a good time chatting with each other.
  • You still need to register, so that we provide a good experience to everybody. If you're privacy minded, feel free to choose any username on Discord you want, we don't insist on your real name.

After you register, you will find an invite link to our Discord server on your registration page. Please ignore our previous invite to the ZuriHac Slack, we won't be using it this year.

Make sure you also head over to out projects page, and have a look at what interests you.

That's it! We are of course doing this for the first time, and for all its downsides we are actually pretty excited about some additional possibilities that a remote event unlocks.

If you have any comments or questions, just reach out to the orgnisers, you will find out contact information below.
